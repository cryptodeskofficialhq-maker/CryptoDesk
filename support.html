Support & Help

CryptoDesk provides structured support through multiple channels. Choose the appropriate method based on your needs.

Before Contacting Support

Most questions can be answered by reviewing existing resources:

  1. Check the FAQ page
  2. Read pinned messages in Telegram channels
  3. Review the Rules and Process pages
  4. Use the CryptoDesk Support bot in Telegram when available

What We Support

CryptoDesk support can help with:

  • Payment and subscription issues
  • Access and authentication problems
  • Technical platform issues
  • Account management questions
  • Policy clarifications
  • Marketplace and rewards inquiries

What We Do NOT Support

CryptoDesk support cannot help with:

  • Trading advice or market predictions
  • Financial or investment guidance
  • Personal trading account issues
  • Exchange-specific problems
  • Fund recovery or management

Support Guidelines

When contacting support:

  • Be clear and specific about your issue
  • Include relevant details (usernames, transaction IDs, etc.)
  • Use the appropriate channel for your question
  • Be patient and respectful
  • Avoid duplicate messages across channels
