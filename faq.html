FAQ

What is CryptoDesk?

CryptoDesk is a private trading community focused on structure, discipline, and long-term development. It is not a public group.

Is CryptoDesk a signal service?

No. CryptoDesk does not provide financial advice or public trade calls. Participation is educational and structural in nature.

Who can join CryptoDesk?

Both new and experienced traders can apply. Acceptance depends on suitability and adherence to rules.

Does CryptoDesk manage funds?

No. CryptoDesk never holds or controls user funds. You always trade your own account independently.

Are results guaranteed?

No. Trading involves risk and no guarantees or promises are made.

How do I get access?

Access details are shared privately after review. Participation may require a subscription.
